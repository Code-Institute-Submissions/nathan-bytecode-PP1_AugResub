Summary of Beliefs

Revival Life Church holds to the 16 Fundemental Truths drafted by the world-wide Penetecostal domnination, Assemblies of God. Arguably the most highlighted of these doctrines would be that Scripture is God inspired, the doctrine of the Trinity, Jesus is the only way to salvation and the baptism of the Holy Spirit.
